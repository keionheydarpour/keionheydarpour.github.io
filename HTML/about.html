Hey There!
My name is Keion Heydarpour, and I'm an undergraduate student at the University of Michigan. I’ve actually only started doing photography around a year ago, but ever since it’s honestly become a passion of mine (I know, that doesn’t sound very original). It's cheesy to say, but photography has made me view the world around me completely differently, and it has genuinely allowed me to appreciate how lucky we are to live in such a beautiful planet. I love to travel and explore, and I am looking forward to capturing all the stuff that I see for the rest of my life.
